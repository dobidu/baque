<objective>
## Goal
Replace ScreenPlaceholder for FX and MIX slots with functional screen components: FxScreen exposes 6 global APVTS-backed knobs; MixScreen shows 16 lane strips (gain fader, pan knob, mute/solo buttons, velocity meter) plus a master strip (gain fader, L/R peak meter), all timer-gated at 30fps.

## Purpose
Phase 10 delivers a playable UI. FX and MIX are the two highest-traffic parameter screens after PERFORM — without them producers can't shape sound or balance levels from the UI. Both screens wire to engine infrastructure already in place (APVTS global FX params, UiCommandQueue per-pad commands, UiStateSnapshot lane_last_velocity + master_peak).

## Output
- `src/ui/fx_screen.h` + `fx_screen.cpp` — 6-knob global FX rack
- `src/ui/mix_screen.h` + `mix_screen.cpp` — 16-strip mixer, timer-gated
- `src/plugin_processor.h` — `getAPVTS()` public getter (needed by SliderAttachment)
- `src/plugin_editor.h/.cpp` — FxScreen + MixScreen constructed in screens_ slots
- `CMakeLists.txt` — 2 new .cpp sources
- `tests/test_fx_mix_screens.cpp` — FX1, MIX1, MIX2
</objective>
